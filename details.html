Our story
Cooking with heart since 2008
What started as a small family kitchen in vishakhapatnam has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.
"Every dish we make carries a story — of tradition, family, and the love of good food."
We source our spices from local farms, our produce from trusted suppliers, and our recipes from generations of culinary wisdom. Nothing artificial. Nothing frozen. Just honest, delicious food.
